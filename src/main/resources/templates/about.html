Why we do what we do.

The Book Club App originated as a group of three who would meet in the Bellevue University library to discuss their favorite books. This small group started to grow as others took notice of the discussion and would chime in.

As this club originated at college they wanted to create a way to keep the discussions going as they moved on with life and careers. That's how the idea behind this app was formed.

The app has since formed into more than just a way to continue discussions. We also created a format that hopefully helps others start book clubs in their own area. Building community through books and diverse discussion.
